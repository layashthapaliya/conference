Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Who can attend the symposium?
The symposium is open to researchers, practitioners, and stakeholders from institutions worldwide who work in the areas of organic waste valorization, anaerobic digestion, resource recovery, and related sustainability topics. Space is limited to 50–60 participants.
Will there be a proceedings publication?
Details on a proceedings volume or journal special issue will be announced. Selected high-quality presentations may be considered for publication in a special issue.
Can I attend only one day?
The fee covers the full two-day event. One-day registration may be available on request — contact the secretariat to enquire.
Is there a student discount?
A reduced student rate may be available. Contact the secretariat with proof of student status to enquire about eligibility.
What language will sessions be in?
All presentations, sessions, and official communications are in English only. No simultaneous translation will be provided.
Will accommodation be arranged?
Accommodation is not included and must be booked independently. The organizing committee may share recommended hotel options near the venue and may negotiate group rates.
