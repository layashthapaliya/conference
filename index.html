About the Symposium

A Focused Forum for
Organic Waste Science

A compact two-day gathering of researchers and stakeholders dedicated to advancing organic waste valorization and forging cross-institutional collaborations.

  • →Share latest research on resource recovery, anaerobic digestion, and valorization
  • →Showcase collaborative outcomes from joint research projects across institutions
  • →Explore links between waste valorization, sustainability metrics, and climate resilience
  • →Establish and strengthen international research partnerships
  • →Plan future joint research and collaboration initiatives

Research Themes

Session Topics

Five thematic sessions spanning the full spectrum of organic waste valorization research, open to contributors from institutions worldwide.

⚗️
Advances in Anaerobic Digestion
Novel AD technologies, process optimization, microbial communities, and reactor innovations for improved biogas yields.
🔄
Organic Waste Valorization Processes
Beyond conventional AD — composting, fermentation, hydrothermal treatment, and other emerging valorization pathways.
♻️
Resource Recovery Technologies
Biogas upgrading, high-value chemicals, nutrient recovery, and digestate valorization strategies.
🦠
Biological Processes Beyond AD
Hybrid systems (AD + composting, AD + microbial electrolysis), emerging biological routes, and bioelectrochemical systems.
🌍
Sustainability & Climate Resilience
LCA, GHG emission reductions, pilot/full-scale case studies, and climate resiliency metrics.
🤝
Collaborative Research
Joint project outcomes, cross-disciplinary initiatives, and establishing new international research partnerships.

Symposium Format

What to Expect

🎤
Keynote Lectures
4 keynote talks by invited senior researchers and experts, opening each day's themes.
🗣️
Oral Presentations
5 plenary sessions with 12–15 minute talks. No parallel sessions — every participant hears every talk.
📋
Poster Session
Day 1 afternoon poster session for detailed one-on-one discussions of ongoing research work.
🌿
Site Visit
Day 2 afternoon: guided tour of a local biogas plant and a Balinese mangrove forest.
🍽️
Welcome Dinner
Day 1 evening dinner with optional Balinese cultural performance — a setting for new collaborations.
🎓
Closing Ceremony
Day 2 ends with a farewell dinner, acknowledgements, and agenda-setting for future joint work.
