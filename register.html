Step-by-Step Guide

How to Register

New to the process? Here's exactly what to expect from abstract submission to confirmed registration.

1
Prepare Your Abstract

Write your abstract (max 200 words) and short biography (max 150 words) in a Word document using Times New Roman 12pt, single spacing. Include a presenter photograph.

2
Submit Your Abstract

Email your document to rr2026registration@gmail.com with the subject line: "Abstract – [Your Name] – [Session]". You will receive a confirmation within 3 working days.

3
Receive Acceptance Email

Once your abstract is reviewed and accepted, you will receive an acceptance notification by email. Do not fill in the registration form until you have received this email.

4
Complete Registration Form

Fill in the registration form above with your personal and institutional details. Select your participation type and preferred session. Submit the form.

5
Receive Payment Details

Within 3 working days, you will receive bank transfer instructions for the USD 300 registration fee. Complete the transfer and send proof of payment by email.

6
Confirmed! 🎉

Once payment is received, your registration is confirmed. You will receive a receipt and an official confirmation email. Welcome to RR2026!

Important: Registrations submitted without a prior abstract acceptance will be considered invalid. Always wait for your acceptance email before filling in the form.
