Getting Here

Travel Information

✈️
By Air
Fly into Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS). Direct flights available from major hubs including Hong Kong (HKG), Seoul (ICN), Singapore (SIN), Tokyo (NRT), and Jakarta (CGK).
🚗
Airport Transfer
~15–20 minutes by car. Grab and Gojek ride-hailing apps are widely available. Estimated fare: IDR 80,000–150,000 (~USD 5–10).
📋
Visa Information
Most nationalities can obtain a Visa on Arrival (VoA) at Bali Airport (~USD 35, 30-day validity). Check Indonesia's official immigration website for your country's requirements.
